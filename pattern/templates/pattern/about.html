About Patternlib

Currently, patternlib is a website for sharing patterns made with TidalCycles (also known as Tidal) code. It may well expand to host other kinds of patterns in the future.

Patternlib was made during a residency at the Open Data Institute in London, as part of the Sound and Music embedded programme. Warm thanks for the amazing support!

Warm thanks also to David Ogborn, Mauro and all the contributors to TidalCycles for helping make this possible.

The source code for this side is available on github under the GNU Public License v3.
